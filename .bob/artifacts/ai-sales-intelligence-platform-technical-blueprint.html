AI Sales Intelligence Platform

Technical blueprint & system design — IBM watsonx challenge

Overview

A full-stack AI-assisted sales platform that ingests account data, enriches it with third-party signals, uses watsonx to prioritize and personalize outreach, and automates execution through SalesLoft — while keeping the rep in control at every step.

Feature Modules

Module 1 — Account & Data Ingestion

1A · Account Import

  • CSV / Excel upload with column mapping UI
  • Paste-in list support (newline-separated)
  • Deduplication on domain / company name
  • Stored in persistent Account table

1B · External Enrichment

  • ZoomInfo API — contacts per account
  • Install base feed — existing product footprint
  • LISN — buyer intent / signal scores
  • Web research — watsonx scrape & summarise
Accounts missing an IT Director-level contact are auto-flagged and optionally dropped before prioritization runs.

Module 2 — Rep Profile

Products & Territory

  • Products sold (multi-select from knowledge base)
  • Territory definition (geo, industry, segment)
  • Target title / persona mapping

Writing Style & Preferences

  • Upload example emails for style extraction
  • Tone sliders (formal ↔ casual, brief ↔ detailed)
  • Automation permission level (0 = approve all → 5 = fully auto)
  • Notification cadence preferences

watsonx.ai Integration Architecture

Prompt Chain for Account Prioritization

Step 1 — Summarize raw signals (install base + LISN + news) into a structured account brief.
Step 2 — Score brief against rep's territory/products using a classification prompt → tier A/B/C/D.
Step 3 — Identify best-fit use-case bucket from knowledge base (RAG over pgvector embeddings).
Step 4 — Generate outreach plan with scheduling constraints.
Step 5 — Draft first-touch email, grounded in account brief + rep writing style.

Knowledge base articles are embedded at ingest time using watsonx.ai embedding endpoint and stored in pgvector. Each email generation step performs a top-5 retrieval for relevant product talking points.

Governance Model

Automation LevelBehavior
0 — ManualAI suggests only. Rep approves every action before anything is sent or scheduled.
1 — AssistedAI drafts emails and plan. Rep reviews a daily digest and approves in bulk.
2 — Semi-autoEmails load into SalesLoft drafts automatically. Rep sends manually.
3 — Auto (business hrs)Emails send automatically during business hours. Rep notified of sends.
4 — Fully autoFull automation. Rep receives weekly summary only. Can override at any time.
Regardless of automation level, all AI-generated content is logged with the originating prompt, model version, and timestamp for auditability.
